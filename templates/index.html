Grilled chicken with provolone, artichoke hearts, and roasted red pesto

Experience the robust flavors of Tuscany with our Tuscan Grilled selections, showcasing perfectly grilled meats or vegetables infused with traditional Tuscan seasonings, offering a hearty and satisfying dining experience.

Ramen

Ramen is a traditional Japanese noodle soup dish consisting of wheat noodles served in a flavorful broth, often accompanied by toppings such as sliced pork, soft-boiled eggs, seaweed, green onions, and other garnishes

Indulge in the authentic taste of Japan with our Ramen selections, featuring aromatic and savory broth combined with perfectly cooked noodles and a variety of toppings, providing a comforting and flavorful bowl of goodness.
